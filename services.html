Forgerock Identity Platform

The ForgeRock Identity Platform combines the best-in-class solutions for authentication, identity governance, access management, and directory services.

Combines best-in-class solutions for authentication, identity governance, access management, and directory services.
Manages the entire identity lifecycle, from user registration to deactivation, ensuring a secure and compliant process.
Enforces granular access control policies to regulate and manage user access based on roles, context, and risk.
Implements adaptive security mechanisms, adjusting authentication and authorization methods based on user behavior and risk profiles.
Seamlessly integrates with external applications, services, and cloud environments using standard protocols (e.g., OAuth, SAML, OpenID Connect).

Scales to meet the needs of both enterprise-level and customer-facing solutions, ensuring flexibility and growth for any organization.

CyberArk

The ForgeRock Identity Platform combines the best-in-class solutions for authentication, identity governance, access management, and directory services.

CyberArk provides Privileged Access Management (PAM) to protect and manage privileged accounts.
CyberArk records and monitors privileged sessions for compliance and security auditing.
The platform enforces least privilege access to minimize unnecessary permissions.
CyberArk detects and responds to threats targeting privileged accounts in real-time.
CyberArk extends security to cloud environments, including AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud.
It integrates with IAM systems to streamline user identity and access management.
Scales to meet the needs of both enterprise-level and customer-facing solutions, ensuring flexibility and growth for any organization.
